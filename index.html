What ExcelAudit Helps You Do

ExcelAudit is a lightweight local tool designed for Excel change tracking, audit trail creation, and spreadsheet version comparison. It helps you see what changed, keep a clear record of modifications, and review Excel files without relying on Excel’s limited built-in history features.

Why Use ExcelAudit Instead of Manual Review?

Manually checking Excel files is slow and unreliable, especially when spreadsheets are updated repeatedly or shared between multiple people. ExcelAudit helps you identify modifications faster, reduce review effort, and keep a clearer record of spreadsheet changes.
