Why Use ExcelAudit Instead of Manual Review?

Manual spreadsheet review is slow, repetitive, and easy to get wrong. When Excel files are updated often, it becomes difficult to reliably identify which cells changed and whether important modifications were missed.

  • Manual comparison takes time
  • Changed values can be overlooked
  • Old values are often lost after saving
  • Reviewing large spreadsheets is error-prone

ExcelAudit helps solve this by recording detected changes in a structured and reviewable format.
