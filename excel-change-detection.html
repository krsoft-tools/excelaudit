Detecting changes in Excel can be difficult when files are updated repeatedly or reviewed by multiple people. Even small spreadsheet edits can affect totals, formulas, reports, or business decisions without being easy to notice.

Why Manual Excel Review Often Fails

Manually comparing spreadsheet versions is slow and unreliable, especially in larger workbooks. Important cell changes can be missed, hidden among hundreds or thousands of values, formulas, or tabs.

ExcelAudit helps you identify differences faster by focusing directly on changed spreadsheet content.

What ExcelAudit Detects

ExcelAudit detects modifications between spreadsheet states and helps you review the actual data changes instead of searching for them manually.

  • Changed cell values
  • Previous and new values
  • Differences between spreadsheet versions
  • Modified worksheet content

When Excel Change Detection Is Useful

  • Reviewing updated business spreadsheets
  • Checking data after external edits
  • Verifying changes before approval
  • Finding unexpected spreadsheet modifications

See Excel Changes More Clearly

Instead of reviewing spreadsheets cell by cell, ExcelAudit gives you a clearer view of what changed and where the modification occurred. This helps reduce review time and makes spreadsheet checks more reliable.
