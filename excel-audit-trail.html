Excel does not provide a reliable audit trail of cell-level changes. Tracking what exactly changed in a spreadsheet can be difficult, especially when working with critical data.

What ExcelAudit does

ExcelAudit records every cell change and creates a tamper-evident audit log. Each change is stored together with a cryptographic hash, making any manipulation detectable.

Use cases

  • Internal audit of spreadsheets
  • Tracking changes in financial files
  • Spreadsheet data integrity verification
  • Detecting unauthorized modifications
