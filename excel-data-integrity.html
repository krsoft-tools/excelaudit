Excel data integrity means being able to trust that spreadsheet data is accurate, reviewable, and not silently altered without detection. This becomes especially important when spreadsheets are used for operations, reporting, compliance, or financial work.

Why Excel Data Integrity Matters

  • Spreadsheet values can be overwritten without trace
  • Errors and unauthorized edits may go unnoticed
  • Shared files often lack reliable history
  • Trust in the data depends on visibility and reviewability

What Helps Protect Data Integrity

A stronger spreadsheet workflow should make it easier to review changes and verify that records were not modified without visibility.

  • Clear tracking of modified cells
  • Old and new values for review
  • A structured record of detected changes
  • Integrity checks for audit records

How ExcelAudit Helps

ExcelAudit helps support Excel data integrity by recording spreadsheet changes and preserving them in a tamper-evident audit log. This gives you a clearer way to review updates and identify when something may have been altered unexpectedly.

It is useful when you need more confidence in spreadsheet records and want a practical way to verify important data changes over time.
