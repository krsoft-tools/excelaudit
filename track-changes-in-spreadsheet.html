If you need to track changes in a spreadsheet, the most important thing is being able to review modified cells quickly and understand how the data was updated.

Why Spreadsheet Change Tracking Matters

  • Important edits can be overlooked
  • Old values are replaced by new ones
  • Spreadsheet history is often incomplete
  • Manual checking is slow and repetitive

What Good Spreadsheet Change Tracking Should Show

A useful spreadsheet change tracking workflow should make it easier to review changes and keep a reliable record of updates.

  • Which cells were modified
  • Old and new values
  • Where changes occurred
  • A clear record of detected edits

How ExcelAudit Helps

ExcelAudit helps you track changes in a spreadsheet by recording detected modifications in a structured audit log. This gives you a clearer view of what changed and helps reduce manual review work.

It is useful when you need a practical way to monitor spreadsheet edits and review changes without relying on cloud systems or complex built-in features.
